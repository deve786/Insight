About
Our insight

INSIGHT, the technical fest hosted by the students of CCSIT of Calicut University is a celebration of intelligence and knowledge to a new world of technology.

W e provides a platform for the students to explore and enhance their technical skills and also a best opportunity to endeavour beyond the monotonous academic curriculum.

This jubilee puts forth a rostrum for 4000+ students from different colleges to participate in seminars, exhibitions, workshops, technical and cultural events. 

The focus area of INSIGHT is MACHINE LEARNING AND CYBER SECURITY

The family of CCSIT is a group of University off-campus centres which offers MCA, M.Sc Computer Science, B.Sc IT programmes. There are 13 centres under CCSIT family which have almost 1100+ students. INSIGHT facilitates a platform for other CCSIT students to gather at the University campus and explore their capabilities

Treasure Hunt

A scavenger hunt is a game in which the organizers prepare a list defining specific items, which the participants seek to gather or complete all items on the list, usually without purchasing them. Usually participants work in small teams, although the rules may allow individuals to participate.

Quiz competition

A quiz is a quick and informal assessment of student knowledge. Quizzes are often used in North American higher education environments to briefly test a students' level of comprehension regarding course material, providing teachers with insights into student progress and any existing knowledge gaps.

Rules and Regulations

Babbage Bugging

Debbuging

In software development, the debugging process begins when a developer locates a code error in a computer program and is able to reproduce it. Debugging is part of the software testing process and is an integral part of the entire software development lifecycle.

BABBAGE BUGGING

Rules and Regulations

  • Time limit for this competition is 1 hour.

Paper presentation

Paper presentation is an event where the participants are required to make a paper about a certain topic or area, and then present it in front of a jury, following which it will be rated and judged based on their performance.
